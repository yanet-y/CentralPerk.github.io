Welcome to the Central Perk Restaurant

" Where Friends and Food Meet."
Grab a seat, relax, and enjoy the good company. Just like in the show, we're here to be your everyday escape. Whether you're craving a delicious cup of coffee, a hearty meal, or simply a place to unwind with friends, you've come to the right place. So, kick back, and let's make some memories together!

Central Perk's Specials

Ross's moist maker sandwich


A towering tribute to Thanksgiving leftovers. Layers of tender turkey, savory stuffing, and cranberry sauce are brought together by the legendary "moist maker" for a sandwich that's as iconic as Ross's paleontology career.

Smelly cat salad


This fresh and flavorful salad is a tribute to Phoebe's unforgettable tune. A delightful mix of greens, crunchy vegetables, and a secret ingredient that will leave you singing its praises.

Unagi rolls


Crispy spring rolls filled with a delicious blend of vegetables and a touch of spice. Just like Ross's concept of "unagi," these rolls will leave you feeling prepared for any culinary adventure.

Pivot burger


This burger is a challenge! Packed with flavor and topped with your choice of cheese, it's sure to have you shouting "Pivot!" with every bite.

The cheesecake on the front door


This decadent cheesecake is as elusive as the one that mysteriously appeared on Chandler's doorstep. Indulge in its rich, creamy perfection and let your taste buds unravel the mystery.

The central perk


A warm and comforting coffee cake, perfect for sharing with friends. Just like the beloved coffee shop, this cake is a gathering place for good times and great flavors.
