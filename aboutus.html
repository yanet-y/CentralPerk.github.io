About Us

Inspired by the enduring bond and heartwarming humor of Friends, we created Central Perk, an ideal restaurant where you can relive cherished memories and forge new friendships. From designing the menu to creating the logo and selecting a Friends-themed color palette, this passion project was born out of love for the iconic sitcom.

Our restaurant is a tribute to the show, bringing to life the cozy apartment on Monica's corner, the laughter-filled Central Perk, and the relatable struggles of the six friends. We aim to provide a welcoming and inclusive community where everyone can feel a sense of belonging, just like the characters in Friends.
